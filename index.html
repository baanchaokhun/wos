09 — Frequently Asked 09 / 09

Questions hotel owners ask first.

How much investment is required?

There's no cost for the first step, and no renovation needed. Most hotels can start right away using their existing rooms and facilities. The details and revenue-sharing model are designed together during Program Design, after the Discovery Call.

How long does the pilot take?

The pilot runs 90 days across three phases: discovery, program design, and launch.

What kind of hotel is this suited for?

We welcome hotels looking to elevate into Healthcare Hospitality. Recommended baseline criteria:
• Enough rooms to support the program (30+ recommended for efficient management)
• Located within easy reach of a partner hospital (especially in the Udon Thani zone connecting to Laos PDR)
• A vision to build a new revenue channel and point of difference, beyond traditional room sales

Is there a cost to join the pilot?

No cost for the first step, and no renovation needed to join the pilot. Any commercial terms are agreed together during Program Design.

What does WOS actually do?

We connect your hotel to hospitals, wellness partners, and cross-border patients, and help you sell and run the packages.

How is the hospital involved?

The hospital provides clinical care and screening; your hotel provides the stay. WOS coordinates both sides.

How does the Laos market fit in?

Udon Thani sits directly across the border from Laos, giving your hotel access to patients and families already travelling for care.

What happens after the 90-day pilot?

We review results together and agree a path to scale the packages that worked best for your property.
